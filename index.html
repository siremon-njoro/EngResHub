About EngRes

EngRes is an educational technology project created to support teachers of English in Senior School. The application provides organized resources that help teachers prepare lessons, access teaching notes, create revision activities, and support learners more effectively.

The platform is designed with teachers as the primary users. It brings together practical English teaching materials in one accessible mobile platform, making lesson preparation faster, easier, and more organized.

Although learners may also benefit from the resources, EngRes is mainly built for teachers who need reliable, structured, and classroom-friendly English teaching materials for Senior School.

The goal of EngRes is to simplify English lesson preparation and provide teachers with convenient access to quality digital teaching resources.

Premium Access

EngRes provides selected free resources and premium teaching materials. Premium access is intended to unlock extended notes, additional teaching resources, advanced revision materials, assessment support content, and other professional tools for teachers of English.

The premium model allows the project to continue developing, updating, and improving English teaching resources for users.

Payments for premium access are intended to be processed through M-Pesa, allowing teachers and other users in Kenya to conveniently unlock paid teaching resources through mobile money.

Business Profile

EngRes is operated as a software development and educational technology project by Simon Njoroge. The project focuses on creating digital tools that support the teaching of English in Senior School.

The platform provides organized teaching notes, revision materials, lesson support resources, assessment tools, and premium educational content through a mobile application.

Nature of Business

Educational technology, mobile application development, teacher resource development, digital learning resources, and software-based educational services.

Primary Service

Development and distribution of digital teaching resources for English language education at Senior School level.

Primary Users

Teachers of English in Senior School who need organized, accessible, and practical resources for lesson preparation and learner support.

Other Beneficiaries

Learners, tutors, schools, tuition centres, and independent users may also benefit from the educational resources available on the platform.

Revenue Model

Free access to selected resources, with paid access to premium teaching materials, advanced revision resources, and additional educational tools.

Payment Method

Premium access is intended to be supported through M-Pesa payments for convenient mobile money transactions in Kenya.

Our Mission
