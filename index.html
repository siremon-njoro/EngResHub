EngRes

EngRes is an educational mobile application designed to help students and teachers improve English language learning through notes, exercises, assessments, revision materials, and premium educational content.

Features

  • English Language Notes
  • Revision Exercises
  • Assessment Tools
  • Premium Learning Resources
  • Teacher Support Materials

Target Users

  • Secondary School Students
  • Teachers
  • Independent Learners

Business Model

Users can access basic features free of charge. Premium content and advanced features are unlocked through paid subscriptions.
